Australian Rissoles




Description:

Australian rissoles are meat patties that are grilled on the BBQ. Depending on the recipe, these patties can include grated vegetables.

Prep Time 10 mins + Cook Time 10 mins = Total Time 20 mins

Ingredients:

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 onion , finely chopped
  • 90 g bread crumbs
  • Â½ teaspoon dried herbs
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg , beaten
  • Flour

Preperation:

  1. Mix all the ingredients together with the exception of the flour.
  2. Shape into balls and flatten slightly. Rissoles should be about 2 inches (5cm) in diameter for a main course, smaller if they are served as appetizers.
  3. Put the flour in a bag, then add 2 rissoles at a time.
  4. Stir gently until rissoles are covered with flour. Shake off excess flour before cooking.
  5. Cook on a grill or barbecue for about 8 to 10 minutes on each side.
